About Me

Just some background info on me.

I grew up in Chandler, AZ where I loved the great local food, beautiful hikes, and unique scenery. In Robotics Club, which competed in the First Robotics Competition, I gained experience programming as the Head Programmer and integrating designs as the Team Captain. Through my hard work in effort in Boy Scouts I also completed my Eagle Scout rank. I'm now attending college at the University of Notre Dame where I'm pursuing a degree in Computer Engineering and minor in Engineering Corporate Practice. I am always excited to learn new things and will do everything I can to not let opportunity slip through my fingers.
